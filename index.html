Dr. Hamid Fazal
MBBS, ECFMG Certified, PGD (Nutrition)

Dr. Hamid Fazal is a physician working at the intersection of internal medicine, psychiatry, and community mental health. His clinical experience spans critical care, chronic disease management, CKD and dialysis services, and psychiatric practice. His academic interests include neuropsychiatry, cognitive aging, dementia, nutrition in chronic disease, and the complex relationship between physical and mental health.
